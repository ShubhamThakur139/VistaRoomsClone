About ,

Located amidst scenic mountains, Pine Estate is a stunning home in the quaint town of Shimla. Featuring elegant interiors with a hint of colonial splashed across the house, this 130-year old heritage property has been a witness to several books written by T P Russell Stracey. With gorgeous lawns that make for ideal breakfast spots, al fresco dining overlooking the surrounding scenery and the attached balconies offering the perfect vantage point, this holiday home should definitely be next on your list!

FOOD

- All meals can be provided in-house on an a la carte basis.

- Consumption of non-vegetarian food is allowed.

GUEST ACCESS

The guests have access to their rooms and common areas, except the kitchen. In response to the COVID-19 pandemic, guests may have restricted access to any shared amenities.

INTERACTION WITH GUESTS

The host is present at the property and is always accessible. A trained and responsible caretaker is present at the property to resolve any concerns and help you have a perfect stay.

STAFF ACCOMMODATION

We are unable to provide accommodation for your personal staff.

ACTIVITIES

Within the property, guests can carry and play indoor games like cards and ludo. Guests can take a peaceful walk in the lawn and embrace the beauty around or engage in various outdoor games like badminton. They can relish a scrumptious barbecue meal and request for a bonfire to be lit up.

ADDITIONAL COSTS

- Barbecue kit is available at an additional cost of Rs. 500 per use.

- Bonfire can be arranged at an additional cost of Rs. 500 per hour.

- The jacuzzi can be used at an additional cost of Rs. 2000 for a duration of 2 hours.

- Prices may vary subject to availability and peak season rates.
